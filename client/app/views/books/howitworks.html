How it Works

What is OpenLibrary

OpenLibrary helps you create a personal Library catalog of books. Books you 've read and books you are willing to give. Also, lets you get books you are looking for from others.

Search for a book

Account creating is only required when adding books to your personal Library or when you want to get a book. Just click on "Search books" and enter the words into the Search box. The result list will show you matches. Click on the edition you want.

Easy to join

To sign up visit our home page and create an account using your e-mail. To sign in, fill in your e-mail and password. Then click the button "Sign me in!". There' s no cost to join the website!

What' s on your Library

Adding books is easy. After searching a book, click on the edition you want to add it to your profile by clicking the buttons "I read it" or "I rent it". OpenLibrary gets its books from Goooglebooks.

Ask for a book

Once you find a tittle, click the "I want it" button. OpenLibrary then gives you a list of the book owners e-mails, to contact with and ask the book you 're interesting in.

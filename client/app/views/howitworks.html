How it Works

What is OpenLibrary

OpenLibrary helps you create a personal Library catalogue of books.

Books you 've read and books you are willing to give. Also, it lets you get books you are looking for from others.

Search for a book

Account creating is only required when adding books to your personal Library or when you want to barrow a book.

Just click on "Search books" and enter the keywords into the Search box. The result list will show you matches. Click on the edition you want.

Easy to join

To sign up visit our home page and create an account using your e-mail.

To sign in, fill in your e-mail and password. Then click the button "Sign me in!". There' s no cost to join the website!

What' s on your Library

Adding books is easy.

After searching a book, click on the edition you want. To add it to your profile just click the buttons "I read it" or "I rent it".

Ask for a book

Once you find a tittle, click the "I want it" button.

Then OpenLibrary gives you a list of the book owners' e-mails, to contact with and ask the book you 're interested in.
